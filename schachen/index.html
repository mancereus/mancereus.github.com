Chess Me - 2nd edition

ChessMe is a fast paced chess variant without a board. The first edition was sold out and got great reviews in many countries.
We proudly present the second edition with slighty improved layout and rules and additional french and italian rules.

ChessMe is published by Mücke Spiele and can be bought here. There is also a limited special offer to celebrate the second edition here.
